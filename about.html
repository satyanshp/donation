about us
Covid-Donation is a nonprofit that supports other nonprofits by connecting them to donors and companies. We've helped trusted, community-led organizations access the tools, training, and support they need to make our world a better place.
Safe + Easy Donations
We help donors make safe and easy Indian tax-deductible donations to vetted, locally-driven organizations around the world. Donations are tax-deductible in the India, India taxpayers can give in GBP and claim an extra 25% if Gift Aid eligible. We're so sure you'll love giving through Covid-Donation, we offer a donation satisfaction guarantee.
